Setup

After installing the picogen run the folloing commands to initialize picogen.

$ mkdir <project_folder>
$ cd <project_folder>
$ picogen init

Once initialized, here’s what your project folder will look like:

.
└──src
├── _component/
├── _create/
├── _data/
├── _static/
├── _temlate/
├── pages/
└── posts/
